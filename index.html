Library

Library of PICT is well known everywhere for its richest sources of information. The Library is committed to respond to the needs of users by providing well-organized and up-to-date collection. All the books in the library are arranged by following internationally accepted Classification Scheme i.e. Dewey Decimal Classification. It offers various services to the users. Library has a very spacious Reading Hall with very good infrastructure which is quite comfortable and conducive to reading & learning.

Digital Library

PICT digital Library is separately located on the mezzanine floor of the Central Library. 12 computers having multimedia facility are kept for accessing the contents of the Digital Library, the contents can be accessed from anywhere within the campus and are available 24*7.

Network Attached Storage (NAS): NPTEL Video lectures, project reports, question papers, syllabus, etc. are available on NAS which can also be accessed anywhere and anytime within the campus and are available 24*7.
